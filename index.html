Bacterial Counts

Bacterial counts at VP-003. In order to determine whether Chlorine released during the bio-remediation of TCE/PCE is a limiting factor, we measured the bacterial counts at two points seperated by 70 hours. The sustained population count indicates that increase in levels of Chlorine due to bio-remediation of PCE and TCE are not impacting the bacterial population in VaporRemed. This observation presents a clear evidence of bio-remediation taking place; an observation that is further backed by the reduction in contamination levels is indoor air and sub slab areas.
