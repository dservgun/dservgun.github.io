Bacterial Counts

Bacterial counts at VP-003. In order to determine whether Chlorine released during the bio-remediation of TCE/PCE is a limiting factor, we measured the bacterial counts at two points seperated by 70 hours. 3/23/2020 - 14, 800, 000 CFU/ml 3/25/2020 - 15, 300, 000 CFU/ml The sustained population count indicates that increase in levels of Chlorine due to bio-remediation of PCE and TCE are not impacting the bacterial population in VaporRemed.
